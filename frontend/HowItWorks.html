How DestinAI Works

Discovering your next destination is simple. DestinAI offers two personalized paths to your perfect trip.

First, you choose whether you want a random city suggestion or to go to a city based on your own criteria.

The "I Feel Lucky Today" Flow

  1. If you click the "I feel lucky today" button, our system will suggest a random city from our database.
  2. A text box will then appear. Enter information such as your dream vacation and the dates you want to travel.
  3. Our system will then provide you with a personalized vacation plan based on this initial random city suggestion and your input.

The "I Want to Choose Myself" Flow

  1. If you want to choose a city based on your own criteria, we will ask you for information such as your budget, vacation schedule (dates), and preferred vacation types.
  2. Our AI model will then suggest 3 cities for you. You can choose any of these cities.
  3. Finally, a personalized vacation plan will be created for you based on this selected city and your detailed information.
